## The Gold Star

When Matt recovered from his first bout of depression, his psychiatrist gave him a gold star.

Around the same time, he published his first piece in the Anniston Star.

Neither of them had any idea what was coming next — the mania, the bipolar diagnosis, the second depression, the loss of the farm, the loss of the ministry.

**Why this matters:** The dramatic irony is the point. A small moment of triumph — a literal gold star — right before the ground fell out again. Not inspirational. Not resolved. Just honest and specific and heavy with what neither of them knew.
