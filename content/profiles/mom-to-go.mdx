## The Music

Before Jean was a caterer, she was a classically trained soprano. Music education degree from JSU, then a master's in music. Over thirty productions. Lead operatic roles. Handel's *Messiah*. Orff's *Carmina Burana*. After graduating, she came back to choreograph and lead the soprano section of JSU's Encore! Show Choir for seven years. In 2021, JSU hired her as director of the whole program. She called it her dream job. {/* VERIFY IN INTERVIEW: The JSU article is from 2021. May no longer be directing Encore! as of 2026. Don't state as current fact — ask question 5. */}

She and her husband Steven — also a JSU music grad, a percussionist who plays with a reggae band called The Natti Love Joys — founded The Music Box on Noble Street in Anniston. They've been teaching kids voice, drums, guitar, violin, piano for over a decade. {/* INTERVIEW GAP: When exactly did they open? What was the vision? How many students? */}
