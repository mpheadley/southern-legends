## How He Got to United Way

- Knew the director of United Way through his church
- Asked for an opportunity to get out of the house
- Director hired him for two fall fundraising campaigns — ~25 hours/week for 3 months each
- Working alongside the fundraising director (who now works at Eastman)
- In that work, his eyes opened: talking to business owners, learning about the struggles people face
- He'd missed it — grew up in poverty but didn't know it. United Way made it visible.
