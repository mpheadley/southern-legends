# Anniston Star — Main Street Anniston expands GETUP program after successful first class
*By Bill Wilson, Star Staff Writer — March 11, 2025*
*Source: annistonstar.com (subscriber access)*
*Relevant to Justin Hall profile — he is a GETUP graduate and provides a testimonial*

---

Following the success of its inaugural session, Main Street Anniston has announced the return of GETUP Anniston, a free business education program designed to support aspiring entrepreneurs and small business owners.

[Full article text about the GETUP program — 8-week course covering business planning, financial literacy, digital marketing, legal considerations. Spring 2025 session April 3 – July 10, biweekly Thursdays, Anniston City Meeting Center, 1615 Noble St.]

---

## Justin Hall Testimonial (key excerpt)

Justin Hall, a recent GETUP graduate, said the program is an excellent resource for those looking to start a business in downtown Anniston. Hall co-owns Old Noble, a gift shop in downtown Anniston.

"Each class is broken down step by step on starting your business," Hall said. "You are provided with resources from realtors to even JSU to help your business become successful."

Hall said he learned about grant opportunities and business tools he was unaware of before attending the program.

"I highly recommend anyone wanting to open a business to attend the GETUP program," he said.
