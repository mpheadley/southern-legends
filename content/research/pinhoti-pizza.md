# Pinhoti Pizza Company — Research Notes

## Facts
- **Owners:** Maggie and Kevin Cunningham
- **Address:** 107 South Main Street, Piedmont, AL 36272
- **Hours:** Thu 11–8, Fri–Sat 11–9, Sun 11–7. Closed Mon–Wed.
- **Website:** pinhotipizzacompany.com
- **Opened:** November 2024
- **Named after:** Pinhoti Trail (long-distance hiking trail connecting to Appalachian Trail in GA)
- **Location significance:** First restaurant in Piedmont located directly on the Chief Ladiga Trail, at intersection of trail and Main Street
- **Oven:** Six-foot Woodstone brick oven
- **Dough:** House-made, unbleached flour
- **Sauce:** Marinara from whole tomatoes, no preservatives, no citric acid
- **Signature item:** "Pied-Monster" (loaded, meaty pizza)
- **Interior:** Trail maps, kayaks, bikes, backpacking gear as decor
- **Patio:** Directly on the Chief Ladiga Trail
- **Trail economy context:** Part of the $9.7M economic impact (JSU study) from Chief Ladiga Trail
- **Nearby businesses:** Elevated Grounds (coffee), Ladiga Trail Trikes, Main Street South vendors, Eubanks Welcome Center
