# The Farm Sale
*Journal entry — raw material*

---

## Matt's words

We came home and the house was put on the market. I stopped going outside. Heather scheduled a yard sale to sell off our farm equipment. I couldn't help. I was frozen, overwhelmed.

I was walking in circles, dragging my feet through going through our stuff to sell or trash or give away. I felt worried that I was damaging the kids.

---

## Notes
- The yard sale Heather had to run alone — Matt couldn't help
- Walking in circles. Frozen. That's the image.
- The fear of damaging the kids — not written into yet, but it's there
- This is about what it meant to lose the farm *that way* — not in a clean ending but in a yard sale while you stood inside
