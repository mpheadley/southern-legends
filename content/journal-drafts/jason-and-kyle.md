## Matt's words

Jason Wright is a 20-year friend. I worked with him as his boss for four years at the church. He took over as music director when I left.

Kyle took over as associate pastor when I left.

I didn't reach out to anyone during the silence. Jason called to check on me. That was the whole of it.

Kyle visited the day I went to UAB the first time — before I refused admission and went home, before I asked to go back the next day. He had preached a sermon earlier that year about his own hospitalization. Called it a "vacation with grippy socks." That sermon was the thing that made me willing to go.
