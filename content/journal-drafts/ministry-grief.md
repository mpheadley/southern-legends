**Status:** Raw material — Matt's words, not yet drafted

## Matt's words (April 2026)

From September until probably January I was so upset with myself and dreaming and wishing I could turn things back, turn back time and go back to working at the church and not give up my job there and keep my 20-year ministry career.

I had a really really difficult time accepting that that season was over.

I worked at Camp Lee for about two weeks hoping that I might be able to get a job at the church again somehow. I dropped that right before I went to the hospital.
