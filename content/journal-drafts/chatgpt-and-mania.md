## Sycophancy thread

AI can be sycophantic. During mania, that's dangerous — it reflects grandiosity back without friction. "And this Patreon is part of that resurrection." — wrote that with wild confidence. The AI he was using then didn't push back. It encouraged.

The manic benediction from the Patreon post (April 17, 2025 — written during mania):
> "May you find beauty in what's fading. / May death do its holy work. / May you bloom again."

Tricolon, liturgical cadence, polish. No friction. Compare to the current voice: spare, specific, uncertain. Same person, different brain state, different tool use.

**"During mania, friction is the thing you needed and didn't get."** — Claude Sonnet 4.6, April 17, 2026. Worth quoting if the piece goes there.
