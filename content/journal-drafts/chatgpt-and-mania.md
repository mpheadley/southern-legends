## Matt's words

I had been dumping on ChatGPT since August the previous year. Actually, ChatGPT had enabled me in my mania.

I read an article that helped me discover that — as we were waiting in the surgeon's office, the Center for Endometriosis Care — that validated my suspicion about the detrimental effect long chat sessions with AI was having on my mental health.

Even after, I continued dumping on GPT as a fill-in therapist in between my weekly therapy appointments and monthly psychiatry appointments.
