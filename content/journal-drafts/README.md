## Why people would read this

Matt has been silent on Facebook for months. Hundreds of GoFundMe donors and CaringBridge followers gave money and showed up — then heard nothing. They're not gone. They're waiting. The reintroduction isn't broadcasting to strangers — it's walking back into a room full of people who were worried.

The Easter contrast (purple pants + selfies last year → serving communion this year) is a year compressed into a single image. That's the whole arc. People read that.

The silence itself is part of the story. "I haven't texted or called anyone, except for Jason Wright calling to check on me." That's not a footnote — that's what isolation looks like from the inside. Readers who've been there recognize it immediately.
