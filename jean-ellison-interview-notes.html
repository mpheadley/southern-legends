Jean Ellison — Mom-To-Go

Wednesday April 1, Noon (phone)

Warm-Up (first 2-3 min)

  • We were in Encore! together — what are you up to with JSU these days?
  • I see The Music Box moved — how's the new space?
  • How are the kids?

Questions

  1. How did Mom-To-Go get started? You've got The Music Box, The Treatment — why add catering?
  2. Where does the name come from?
  3. What do you cook that people keep coming back for? Follow up: "I saw you were selling frozen meals at Old Noble — what's that about?"
  4. What do you want people to feel when they eat your food?
  5. Does Steven help with the catering or is that yours?
  6. What's been the hardest part of running all of this at once?

Backup (if time)

  • The Music Box building got sold after 11 years — what was that like?
  • You've got 535 people on Facebook and no website. How do people find you?
Reminders
  • If she goes somewhere interesting, drop the list and follow her
  • Write down 2-3 strongest impressions right after the call
  • Ask if she's OK being recorded (if you want to)
  • End with: "Is there anything you want people to know that I haven't asked?"
